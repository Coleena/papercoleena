Overture

History

The theme that starts every live, every event, even the encore. Overture is a remix of Ikuze! Kaitou Shoujo's bridge by Maeyamada Kenichi, composer of the original song. Ikuze! Kaitou Shoujo marked Momoclo's major debut and was their breakout single, reaching the top of the daily Oricon rankings. Definitely their most iconic song to date, serving as a fitting opening to their performances.

Maeyamada does all the voices, too. He commented that he composed Overture to sound like a pro-wrestling entrance, a sport which Momoclo took heavy influence from (thanks to Kawakami) in their early days. According to him, Overture took about 2.5 hours to compose.

Fans have been asking for one since forever, but there is no CD version.
